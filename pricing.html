Frequently Asked Questions

How do I subscribe?
Click any Subscribe button above, or open ArtiMod's profile in your Discord server and click Subscribe. Payments are processed securely by Discord.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel through Discord at any time. Your server stays on the paid plan until the end of the billing period, then reverts to Free.
Does the free tier expire?
Never. Free is genuinely free forever with no credit card and no time limit.
Can I use ArtiMod on multiple servers?
Yes. Each server requires its own subscription. The free tier is available on unlimited servers.
Who handles billing issues or refunds?
Subscriptions are processed by Discord. For billing disputes or refunds, contact Discord support directly. ArtiMod does not handle payments.
Will ArtiMod auto-ban my members?
Never. ArtiMod removes messages and alerts your mod team with suggested actions. Every punitive action beyond message deletion requires a human moderator to execute.
