Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026  ·  Effective immediately

01 Overview

ArtiMod is committed to being transparent about what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used. This policy explains our data practices in plain language.

ArtiMod does not sell your data. We do not serve ads. We collect only what is necessary for the bot to function.

02 What Data We Collect

ArtiMod collects and stores the following data on a per-server basis:

Data Why We Collect It Retention
Guild (server) ID To store your server's configuration settings Until bot is removed
User IDs To track warnings and moderation actions per user Until cleared or bot removed
User display names For human-readable moderation logs Until cleared or bot removed
Message content Sent to AI for moderation analysis; stored in incident log 90 days rolling
Channel IDs To identify where incidents occurred Until bot removed
Moderation actions Warnings, timeouts, bans — logged for server admin review Until cleared or bot removed
Server configuration Log channel, exempt roles, word lists, settings Until bot removed

03 What We Do NOT Collect

  • Messages that do not trigger moderation checks
  • Direct messages sent to users (only embeds sent by the bot)
  • Voice channel activity
  • Email addresses, IP addresses, or any personal contact information
  • Payment information of any kind
  • Any data from users in servers where ArtiMod is not present

04 How Message Content Is Used

When a message triggers a moderation check (contains a flagged word or link), ArtiMod sends the message content and surrounding conversation context to Anthropic's Claude API for analysis. This is necessary for the bot to make context-aware moderation decisions.

  • Only messages that trigger a moderation check are sent to the AI
  • Context messages (surrounding conversation) are sent to improve accuracy
  • Anthropic processes this data according to their own privacy policy
  • Message content from flagged incidents is stored in our database for moderator review
  • Incident logs are retained for 90 days and then purged automatically

You can review Anthropic's privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy to understand how they handle data sent to their API.

05 Data Storage & Security

All ArtiMod data is stored in a SQLite database hosted on Railway's cloud infrastructure. Railway provides industry-standard security practices including encrypted storage and secure access controls.

Data is stored only for as long as necessary to provide the service. When you remove ArtiMod from your server, your server's data is eligible for deletion within 30 days.

06 Data Sharing

We share data with the following third parties, solely for the purpose of operating the service:

  • Anthropic — Message content sent for AI moderation analysis
  • Railway — Infrastructure provider hosting our database and bot runtime
  • Discord — The platform through which the bot operates

We do not share data with advertisers, data brokers, analytics companies, or any other third parties.

07 Your Rights

As a server administrator, you have the following rights regarding data ArtiMod stores for your server:

  • Access — Use /incidents and /warnings to view stored moderation data
  • Deletion — Use /clearwarnings to remove warning records for specific users
  • Full deletion — Remove ArtiMod from your server; your data will be purged within 30 days

Individual users who believe their data was collected in error may contact the server administrator, who has the tools to review and clear records.

08 Children's Privacy

ArtiMod is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has had data collected through ArtiMod, please contact us so we can address it.

Discord's own Terms of Service require users to be at least 13 years old. ArtiMod operates within this framework.

09 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of ArtiMod after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions about your data?

Reach out via Discord if you have any questions or requests regarding your data.
